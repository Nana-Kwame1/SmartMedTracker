About SmartMedTracker

SmartMedTracker is a mobile and web-based application designed to help users manage their medications, appointments, and health records efficiently. It aims to improve adherence to medical regimens and reduce the risk of health complications.

Key Features

  • Personalized Medication Reminders
  • Appointment Management with Calendar Integration
  • Secure Health Record Storage
  • Insights Dashboard with Visual Analytics
  • Caregiver Access
